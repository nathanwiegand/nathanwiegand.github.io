## Softmax observations

$$
\text{softmax}(z_i) = \frac{e^{z_i}}{\sum_{j=1}^{N} e^{z_j}}
$$

Consider the 2-term case:
$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{softmax}(z_0) &= \frac{e^{z_0}}{e^{z_0} + e^{z_1}}
                    &= \frac{e^{-z_0}}{e^{-z_0}}\frac{e^{z_0}}{e^{z_0} + e^{z_1}}
                    &= \frac{1}{1 + e^{z_1-z_0}}
\end{aligned}
$$
